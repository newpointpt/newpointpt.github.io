What We Do

Our Service Given Physical Therapy By Expert.

Geriatric Rehabilitation

Our fall risk assessment, fall prevention program and balance treatmnet are designed to help our patients achieve their functional goals in and outside the community.

Neurologic Rehabilitation

We provide therapeutic boxing exercises regimen including agility, speed, endurance accuracy, hand-eye coordination, footwork for our neurological patients.

Vestibular Rehabilitation

Our certified vestibular physical therapists will provide treatment not limitted to Benigh Paraxusmal Positional Vertigo (BPPB), Unilateral Vestibular Hypofunction (UVH), Vestibular Neuritis.

Pre Surgical

Our goal is to set all of our patients up for success before surgery. This starts with ensuring that patients are stronger, more functional before any surgeries.

Post Surgical

Our physical Therapists work with you throughout the whole post surgical rehabilitation process including but not limited to improve range of motion, stretching, soft tissue mobilization.

Sport Injuries

We know how important it is to get you back to your sport. No matter if you are an amateur or professional athletes, we are right here for you.

Orthopedic Physical Therapy

Physical Therapy is the most concervative and nature way of musculoskeletal condition. We will assist you to decrease pain and get back to your regular life.

Work Injuries

We will work with you get back to work as soon as possible. Our Physical Therapy designs the right treatment fit best for your work condition.
